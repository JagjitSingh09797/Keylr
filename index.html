Billing setup

Choose how you'd like to pay for services on Keylr.

A payment card is required to activate your account
Once your account is approved, you'll be asked to add a card on file. This is used for pay-upfront jobs. Your card is never charged without your approval.
Billing Cycles Require Approval
If you select a billing cycle (fortnightly or monthly), we will review your account and contact you to approve and activate your Direct Debit. Until then, your account operates on pay-upfront.
What's your preferred billing cycle?
Pay upfront
Default · Instant

Approval required

Monthly (4 weeks)
Approval required
Single monthly consolidated invoice via Direct Debit. Most common for established operators. Our team will contact you to activate.
About Supplier Deposits
Some suppliers may request a deposit for certain jobs. In these cases Keylr generates an option to pay the deposit upfront and leaves the remaining amount for your billing cycle. This is always shown clearly before you confirm a job.
Billing Cycle Approval Process
1 Your preferred billing cycle is noted when you submit your profile
2 Keylr reviews your account — one of our team members will contact you directly
3 Once approved, your Direct Debit is activated and your billing cycle goes live
4 You can book services on pay-upfront immediately while approval is in progress
